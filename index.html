How Music Changes Your DNA

May 09, 2024

Solfeggio frequencies are a set of ancient musical tones used in sacred music, chants, and spiritual practices. They are believed to have healing and transformative properties, with each frequency corresponding to specific spiritual, emotional, or physical benefits. The traditional solfeggio scale consists of six main frequencies:

  • 396 Hz: Liberating guilt and fear
  • 417 Hz: Undoing situations and facilitating change
  • 528 Hz: Transformation and miracles
  • 639 Hz: Connecting relationships
  • 741 Hz: Expression and solutions
  • 852 Hz: Awakening intuition
